Why Wooden Valhalla

No Reverb listings.
No surprises.

You've been burned before. A refin hidden in bad lighting. Weight relief buried in the fine print. A seller who answers "great condition" to every question. That's not how this works.

01
Real Documentation

Every guitar gets 10+ photos shot in natural light — body front and back, headstock, frets, hardware, and anything worth noting. Plus video of it being played. No tricks, no hiding the ugly stuff.

02
Honest Condition Notes

If there's a scratch, you'll know. If the frets are worn, I'll tell you how much life is left. If a pickup was swapped at some point, that's in the description. You shouldn't have to dig for it.

03
A Track Record You Can See

The archive of past guitars isn't just nostalgia — it shows you the kind of instruments I'm drawn to and what I look for. Taste is trust. See what's passed through here and make your own call.

04
Direct. No Middleman.

You're not submitting a ticket. You're reaching a person who's played every guitar on this site and can answer actual questions about feel, tone, neck profile, and condition.

Looking for something specific?

Tell me the year, the finish, the weight range — the more specific, the better. If it's come through here or I know where one is, I'll let you know.
